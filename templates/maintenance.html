How Maintenance Affects Skooty Life

Regular maintenance significantly contributes to the extended life of your skooty by:

  • Preventing premature wear and tear
  • Preserving engine performance
  • Ensuring proper functioning of key components
  • Increasing overall reliability

Tips to Maintain Your Skooty

  • Regularly check and maintain tire pressure
  • Change engine oil and filters as per the manufacturer's recommendations
  • Inspect brake pads and replace if necessary
  • Keep the battery terminals clean and secure
  • Clean and lubricate the chain regularly
  • Store your skooty in a cool and dry place
